RETURNS:

We will always do our best to ensure that you receive the right product ordered. Sometimes, however, mistakes do happen and we are keen to resolve them for you as quickly as possible. 

We urge you to check your order before signing off our delivery driver. If any perishable item is defective, you may return it to the driver before they leave. Once the driver leaves, all perishable items are non-refundable. Refunds will always be processed by the original payment method.
PAYMENT REFUND:

When returning goods to our driver, refunds will be paid onto the same method of payment used on the original order. It takes us between 5-10 days to process the refund. The refund value will be for the price paid when the order was placed. Refunds will only be processed once the item is assessed and confirmed it to be faulty/unsatisfactory.

RETURNING PRODUCTS AND PROOF OF PURCHASE:

All customers will receive a proof of purchase email when their order has been dispatched. Please use this if you wish to return an item to one of our stores. Our staff are unable to process returns without proof of purchase.

MONEY BACK GUARANTEE TERMS AND CONDITIONS:

Our Money Back Guarantee is essentially a simple guarantee that, if the item is defective and you are not satisfied with our product, a refund will be made. We are proud and confident about the quality of our products; therefore, we offer you this guarantee for a piece of mind.
